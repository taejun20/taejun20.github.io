Taejun Kim
Postdoctoral Fellow, KAIST

I am a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST, working at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Previously, I completed my Ph.D., M.S., and B.S. in Computer Science at KAIST, advised by Geehyuk Lee.

My research designs, builds, and evaluates novel and practical interactive technologies that leverage machine learning on mobile and wearable devices, grounded in HCI methodologies and user-centered evaluation. Broadly, my work bridges HCI, machine learning, ubiquitous computing, and extended reality. I publish papers at top-tier venues, always aiming at real-world impacts.

I'm on the job market for tenure-track faculty positions or full-time industry research and engineering positions. Please feel free to reach out, I would be happy to chat.
