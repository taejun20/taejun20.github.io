I am a 4th year PhD student at HCI Lab in KAIST, working with Professor Geehyuk Lee. My curiosity lies in discovering the maximum potential of using our eyes for computer input - "How well can we use our eyes?", which led me to research the opportunities and challenges of utilizing gaze for human-computer interaction. Aside from my primary interest, I also have a fascination with Haptics.
